[03] About me

I make complex, technical products feel human.

Based in Los Angeles. I design for the messy, high-stakes parts of software: the systems that run data centers, the AI tools teams rely on, the consoles where a wrong click costs real money. I started in enterprise finance, moved into big tech at Amazon and Meta, and now take select freelance and consulting work.

Built for the hard problems

I work on technical products most designers avoid. Infrastructure, internal AI tooling, operator software. The complexity is the point.

Design and code, one person

I design it and I ship it. That cuts a normal discovery-to-build loop from quarters to weeks, with no handoff in between.

Enterprise depth, startup speed

Big company rigor without the big company pace. I scale a design system or stand up a scrappy MVP, whatever the work needs.
