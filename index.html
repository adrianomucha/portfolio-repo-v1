I'm a designer exploring the space where systems, AI, and usability meet.

Over the years, I've worked across some of the most complex systems at Meta, Amazon, and Microsoft, shaping products that help people work faster, think clearly, and feel more connected to what they're building.

I live in Los Angeles, where I find ideas while longboarding along the coast or during slow mornings at a local café. I'm driven by design that feels alive — the kind that blends logic with emotion and tools with experience.

Designing with Purpose, Not Just Pixels

I love creating functional, impactful experiences — whether for consumers or engineers — making even complex tools intuitive and enjoyable to use.

Shipping Fast & Iterating

I believe in momentum. I work quickly, get real feedback, and refine — ensuring designs don't just look good but drive results.

Startup Agility with Enterprise Expertise

Whether it's scaling a design system or crafting a scrappy MVP, I adapt my approach to fit the needs of the product and team.
